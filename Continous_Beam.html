Step 3: Calculation of Load

Dead Load (DL)

Self weight of beam = 25 × D/1000 × b/1000 × 1

+ Superimposed dead load (if any)

Total factored dead load, Wu_DL = 1.5 × DL

Live Load (LL)

Total factored live load, Wu_LL = 1.5 × LL

Step 4: Calculation of Moment & Shear Force

Moment

Mu = Coeff_DL × (Wu_DL × l²) + Coeff_LL × (Wu_LL × l²)

Shear Force

Vu = Coeff_DL × (Wu_DL × l) + Coeff_LL × (Wu_LL × l)

Step 6: Computation of Steel Required

Ast_required = 0.5 × fck / fy × [1 − √(1 − 4.6 × Mu / fck × b × d²)] × b × d²
Ast_min = (0.85 × b × d) / fy
Area of 1-φ bar = (π × φ²) / 4
No. of bars = Ast_provided / Area of 1-φ bar

Step 7: Check for Shear

Tv = Vu / (b × d)

Check: τv > τcmax

Pt = (100 × Ast) / (b × d)
Tc = ___ N/mm²
τc_max = ___ N/mm²

Check: τc < τc_max

For Vertical Stirrups (Clause 40.4(a))

Vus = Vu − τc × b × d
Asv = 2 × π × φ² / 4
Sv = 0.87 × fck × Asv × d / Vus

Max Stirrup Spacing (Clause 26.5.1.5)

  • Sv ≤ 0.75d
  • Sv ≤ 300 mm
